Vanilla Bourbon

(42 reviews)

$24.99

Our premium Madagascar Vanilla Bourbon is hand-selected from the finest beans grown in the Sava region. This vanilla has a rich, creamy aroma with sweet, floral undertones and a deep, complex flavor profile.

Ingredients

100% Pure Madagascar Vanilla Planifolia Beans, Ethanol (35% by volume)

Usage

Add 1-2 teaspoons to desserts, beverages, or savory dishes. For aromatherapy, diffuse 3-5 drops in water or apply topically when diluted with a carrier oil.

Store in a cool, dark place. Shake well before use.
